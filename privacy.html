App Privacy Policy

Mykhailo Goloborodko built the Bandura App (iOS and Android) as free mobile application. All applications (furthermore called our "services") are provided at no cost.

This privacy notice informs you how we process your personal data when you use the services.

1 Collection of personal data when using our services

1.1 Download of the mobile app

When you download the mobile app, certain information such as your user name, email address, customer number, time of download, and device code are transferred to the App Store. We do not have control over this data collection and are not responsible for it. Our processing of this information is limited to what is necessary to enable the download of the mobile app onto your device.

1.2 Provision of the services

When using the mobile app, we collect the personal data described below to enable convenient use of the functions. If you want to use our mobile app, we collect the following data that are technically necessary for us to offer you the services and to ensure stability and security:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of the request
  • Time zone difference to Greenwich Mean Time (GMT)
  • Content of the request (specific page)
  • Access Status/HTTP Status Code
  • Amount of data transferred in each case
  • Operating system and its interface

Access to the Service will be provided by means of a personal account or a personalized token for a single session. The legal basis is the provision of the services under the contract, Art. 6 (1) (1) b) GDPR.

1.3 Error Handling

We do not gather information related to errors that occur while using our service directly. However, Google Play and the App Store may provide error handling and reporting functionalities as part of their services. If an error occurs while using our app, Google Play and the App Store may collect certain information to assist with error diagnosis and resolution.

Please refer to their respective privacy policies to understand how they handle and process such data.

Please note that we have no control over the data collected by Google Play or the App Store during the error handling process, and we are not responsible for it.

2 Cookies

Websites commonly use cookies, which are small data files that act as anonymous unique identifiers. These files are sent from the websites to the browsers of the users and then stored on the internal memory of their devices. However, in our service, we do not utilize cookies.

3 Service Providers

To help us provide our services, we may engage third-party companies and individuals for the following reasons:

  • To facilitate our services
  • To provide services on our behalf
  • To perform service-related tasks
  • To assist us in analyzing how our services are used

These third parties may have access to your personal data, but only for the purpose of carrying out the tasks assigned to them on our behalf. They are required to not disclose or use the information for any other purposes.

3.1 Youtube Widget

The YouTube Video Widget is a feature provided by Google LLC that enables service to display video content on its pages. This widget is designed in such a way that YouTube will not store any information or cookies related to Users on Bandura unless they choose to play the video.

The only type of Personal Data that is processed through this widget is Usage Data.

The processing of data through the YouTube Video Widget takes place in the United States and is subject to Google LLC's Privacy Policy. For more information, see Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Expo

We only use the Over-The-Air (OTA) Updates feature from the Expo SDK, which requires the collection of potential crash logs during updates for the mobile app only. However, Expo guarantees that no personal data is transmitted during this process. More information can be found on the Expo documentation page: https://docs.expo.dev/distribution/app-stores/#ios-specific-guidelines.

If we become aware that a child under the age of 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information from our servers.
